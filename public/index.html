Executive Summary

Bloom Skincare's Shopify store is converting at an estimated 1.4–1.8% — well below the 3.2% industry average for beauty brands. This engagement focuses on the two highest-impact levers: a mobile-first product page redesign with integrated social proof and urgency modules, and a streamlined 1-page checkout that reduces friction at the final step. Based on comparable e-commerce redesigns, these changes are projected to increase monthly revenue by 30–45% within 60 days of launch.

Scope of Work

  1. UX & CRO Audit — Full review of current store with heatmap analysis and written recommendations document
  2. Figma Prototype — Mobile-first wireframes and high-fidelity designs for 6 core page templates
  3. Product Page Build — Custom template with sticky add-to-cart, tabbed descriptions, review integration, and urgency module
  4. Collection Page Redesign — Filter/sort UX, lazy-loaded product grid, quick-add functionality
  5. Checkout Optimization — 1-page checkout with trust badges and order bump
  6. Performance Pass — Image compression, lazy loading, targeting 90+ Lighthouse score
  7. QA & Launch Support — Cross-browser testing across 12 device/browser combos + 2-week post-launch monitoring

What's NOT Included

  • Product photography, video content, or copywriting
  • Klaviyo, email, or SMS marketing setup
  • Product data entry or inventory migration
  • Ongoing maintenance after the 2-week support window
