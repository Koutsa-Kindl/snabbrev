This Privacy Policy explains how ScopeAI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website and service at scopeai.io. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Project brief and form inputs — the text you enter (project description, service type, budget, etc.) to generate your proposal. This data is transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate your proposal and is not stored on our servers after the response is returned.
  • Email address — if you voluntarily provide one for a payment receipt. Stored by Stripe for billing purposes.
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Stripe, Inc. We do not collect or store card numbers or payment details.
  • Anonymous usage analytics — page views and anonymized interaction events via Vercel Analytics. No cookies are set. No personal data is collected.
  • Server logs — standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp) for security and debugging. Retained for 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate your proposal by calling Anthropic's Claude API
  • To process your payment via Stripe
  • To send a payment receipt (if email is provided)
  • To analyze aggregate, anonymized usage to improve the service
  • To investigate and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing your project brief to generate and deliver the proposal you paid for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — security logging and fraud prevention.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — anonymized analytics (you can opt out by using an ad blocker or disabling JavaScript).

5. Third-Party Processors

We share data with the following third parties, each acting as a data processor:

  • Anthropic, Inc. (USA) — receives your project brief to generate the proposal. Processed under Anthropic's API Terms and Privacy Policy. Data is used to fulfill the API request and is subject to Anthropic's data retention policies.
  • Stripe, Inc. (USA) — processes all payments. Your payment data is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel, Inc. (USA) — hosts the service and provides cookie-free, anonymized analytics. No personal data is stored in analytics.

International data transfers to the USA are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms.

6. Data Retention

Your project brief is not stored on our servers after the proposal is generated. Payment records are retained by Stripe according to their policies (typically 7 years for financial records). Server logs are deleted after 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email support@scopeai.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

ScopeAI does not use tracking cookies. Vercel Analytics is cookie-free. We may use a browser's localStorage for functional purposes only (e.g., tracking whether you have used your free proposal). No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

9. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to our systems is restricted to essential personnel. We follow industry-standard security practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the website. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
